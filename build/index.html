Professional work and quality service at an affordable cost

  • Free Feasibility Test
  • On-site Project / Paperwork
  • Post-project / Audit

We will answer questions you have about tax credit incentives, and run a feasibility test at no cost to see if your business qualifies for them, and how much benefits can be expected. Even if you are uncertain, do not hesitate to contact us.

Upon completion of feasibility test, if you decide to engage us for the project, we will start gathering detail information, interviewing appropriate personnel, and computing numbers and figures to arrive at the maximized amount of tax credit for you.

Our service is not over until your tax credits are secured. We will assist you with any necessary tasks to do so, especially if your tax credit claims are subject to an audit by IRS.
